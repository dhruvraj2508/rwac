Save Yourself

This ia 4 player luck based game which serves as a means of education on how to protect oneself from COVID 19. The goal of the game is to be the last person left to be uninfected by the virus or if infected be the only one left who could recover. There is one game board,4 coloured coins and one die. The gameboard has 64 even sized circles. Each player is given a different coloured coin to play with. In the board there are36circles which say “stay at home” , 14 circles which say “grocery shopping” ,7 circles which say “medical visit” and 7circles which say “urgent business”.
